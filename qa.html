Q&A

Is MJPlus run by the Michael Jackson Estate or Sony?

No. MJPlus is an independent, fan-run project and is not affiliated with the Michael Jackson Estate or Sony. Our goal is to help preserve Michael Jackson’s legacy.

What is MJPlus?

MJPlus is an online archive dedicated to preserving and documenting rare, unofficial material collected over several decades.

Why are Moonwalker or This Is It not available on MJPlus?

MJPlus does not host or share any officially released content, including Michael Jackson’s official short films and feature productions.

Can I download videos from MJPlus?

No. MJPlus is not a download platform. Our goal is to make the content available to watch in the best possible quality.

Why do some videos not work?

Videos may be updated, replaced, or moved on our servers from time to time. During these updates, a video may be temporarily unavailable. Please check back later and try again.
